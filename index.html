Compliance Requirements & Registration for SMS Services

Welcome to the GWL Telecom Authority (GWTA) Sender ID Registration Portal

This form is designed for Business Entities looking to send SMS messages within Gondwanaland (GWL). Please follow the steps outlined below to ensure your Sender IDs are registered in compliance with GWL's updated telecommunications regulations.

Requirements:

  • You must have a locally incorporated business entity in GWL
  • Specify the Registered Messaging Provider (RMP) through which your messages will be sent
  • Submit a Letter of Authorization (LOA) allowing your chosen Partner RMP to send messages on your behalf from your registered Sender IDs

Instructions:

  • Fill in your business details
  • Register your Sender ID(s)
  • Enter the name of your Registered Messaging Partner
  • Upload a Letter of Authorization to RMP

Upon submission, your application will be reviewed by the GWTA. You will receive confirmation of your registration status once the review is complete.

Thank you for ensuring compliance and facilitating effective communication within GWL.
